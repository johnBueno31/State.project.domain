California

San Diego

San Diego is famous for its sunny weather, beautiful beaches, and laidback culture. It is the southern most tip of California and is definitely worth visiting!

Since San Diego is only 20 minutes away from the Mexican border, some may argue they have the best Mexican food in California.

Sacramento

Sacramento is the capital of California. There is a lot of history to learn about dating all the way back to the gold rush.

Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a bustling city known for its great views, cultural melting pot, roaming movie stars, and great food. Although it may look like a small city, there is so much to do in the city of Angels.
